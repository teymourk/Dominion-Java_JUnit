How To Get Your API Key

Simply go to SoundCloud Developers to get started.

1. Register if you dont already have an account.

2.Create an App name.

You are able to register as many apps as you like.

3.Then You will be presented With Your "Client ID" & "Client Secret"

When you register your app, you will recieve a "Client ID" along with "Client Secret". We wont be needing the "Client Secret" for this tutorial. However, its needed for some of the other uses of the API. Also Make sure to NEVER SHARE YOUR CLIENT ID anywhere else. This is a private key that needs to stay with you and only you. For the purpose of this tutorial I am revealing my key to demonstrate how it works.
